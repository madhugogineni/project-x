
# Inactivity Trigger System

Purpose

Detect prolonged inactivity.

---

## Stages

30 days

Reminder email.

90 days

Escalation email.

120 days

Nominee verification initiated.

---

## Nominee Verification

Nominee must:

Verify identity
Provide legal declaration
Wait through hold window

---

## Hold Window

7 day waiting period before release.

---

## Release

Nominee gains read-only access.
